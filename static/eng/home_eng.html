We allowed the Holocaust to happen each time we didn’t find a way to stop it.

We allowed the Holocaust to happen each time we decided there was something more important to fix, first.

We allowed the Holocaust to happen each time we said: it is someone’s else call.

During the third weekend of 2019 170 people died in the Mediterranean Sea trying to reach Europe.

Who reach Italy suffers continued repression due to the fact of not being Italian.

This reality can’t be accepted anymore.

An outrageous indignation in not enough.

We can not remain astonished but we must remain HUMAN and RESPECTFUL.

For this reason I call a relay fast.

GOAL: obtain from Europe and European governments that immigration is considered with the aim to find inclusive and positive solutions and not just in term of costs and enemies.

METHODS: this is a relay fast to be able to have 170 people on fast for 170 days. If we will be 171 we will try to be 340.

LIMITATIONS: we are social. We have lives, families, passions and jobs. A participant can fast for maximum 5 days during 4 weeks. It is possible to do intermittent fast in blocks of 24 hours. Only 21+ yaers old can partecipate. Any participant accept the complete and total responsability of his own choices.

FAST: 20 people fasting for one day are better than 4 fasting for one. Fasting means abstinence from food. Please keep drinking water and RESPECT your life. Tea, coffee and herbal teas are allowed. For any doubt contact your family doctor. It is possible to participate with other forms of abstinence: smoke and alcohol for example.

SHARING: to the website www.170fasting.com will be shown Name, starting day, ending day, aggragation of total days of fasting and numeber of active participants. Please use #170fasting on your socials during the action.

GET INVOLVED:write Name, starting day, ending day to countmein@170fasting.com. if you do not intend to renounce to food you can:
